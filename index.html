DMI COLLEGE OF ENGINEERING

DMI College of Engineering, founded in 2001 and located in Chennai, is a private, Christian minority institution affiliated with Anna University and approved by the AICTE. The 55-acre campus offers undergraduate and postgraduate engineering and management programs, including BE, B.Tech, ME, and MBA. The college holds accreditation from the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) and focuses on producing competent professionals with exemplary skills and ethical values. Establishment: 2001 Affiliation: Anna University, Chennai Accreditation: AICTE-approved and NBA-accredited Location: Palanchur, Chennai Campus: 55 acres Courses: Offers undergraduate (BE, B.Tech) and postgraduate (ME, MBA) programs Management: Operated by the Sisters of the Society of DMI
